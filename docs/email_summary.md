# Summary of Email to Sandy

This is a profound and deeply personal letter. It bridges a 25-year journey of struggle with a sudden, intense return to *A Course in Miracles*.

## Core ACIM Message in the Letter

The central theme of the letter is the discovery of what it actually means to be a "miracle worker." 

Steven traces this through a specific chain of realisations:
1. **The Premise:** "The sole responsibility of the miracle worker is to accept the Atonement for himself." (T-2:3:65)
2. **The Question:** What exactly is Atonement? (Often defined as "At-one-with God" or "Redemption").
3. **The Breakthrough (Lesson 132):** "I loose the world from all I thought it was." To loose means to free from confinement. This lesson contains three cornerstone ideas:
   - Ideas leave not their source.
   - There is no world!
   - I am as God created me.
4. **The Conclusion:** Atonement is redemption, found in accepting forgiveness from the Holy Spirit. And what are we forgiving? *Nothing.* Because there is no world, only the belief that there is one. 

The ultimate realisation is that salvation is simply the acceptance of peace, which cannot be withheld because "Ideas leave not their source" — we cannot be apart from God.

## Personal Context
The letter frames this ACIM study not as an academic exercise, but as a lifeline. After 2.5 years of unemployment, family upsets, and being in "the darkest place," a recommitment to the Course (one lesson a day, plus deep study) brought about a profound realignment. The conclusion is powerful: *"It took me 25 years of some pretty hard times to figure out that this world has nothing I want... He walks with me always."*
