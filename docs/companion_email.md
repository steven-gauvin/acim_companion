I wanted to share something I've been quietly working on — a small personal app I built called the **A Course in Miracles Personal Companion**.

It started the way most things in my study of the Course start: with a single line.

*"The sole responsibility of the miracle worker is to accept the Atonement for himself."*

That line sent me on a journey. And somewhere along the way, I realised I wanted a place to hold it all — the lessons, the quotes that stopped me in my tracks, the notes I kept writing to myself, the themes I kept returning to. Something I could carry with me and open on my phone in the morning.

So I built it. With a lot of help from an AI assistant who turned out to be a surprisingly good collaborator — patient with my ideas, honest when something wasn't working, and genuinely curious about the material.

---

**Here's what's in it:**

- **Daily Flashcards** — all 365 Workbook lessons, one at a time, with space for personal notes on the back
- **Workbook Library** — every lesson with the full Dee Doyle & Allen Watson commentary, searchable and expandable
- **ACIM Quotes** — a curated collection of my favourite passages from the Course, with a daily featured quote
- **Study Themes** — deeper dives into concepts I keep returning to: Always Remember, Here is the Answer, Angels, Forgiveness, Atonement, and more
- **Meditations & Reference** — supporting material including diagrams, practice instructions, and the Big T / Little t framework
- **Personal Quotes** — a small space for things outside the Course that have meant something to me

It's not a product. It's not finished. It's a living thing — I keep adding to it.

But it's mine, and I wanted you to have the link.

👉 **https://steven-gauvin.github.io/acim_companion/acim_companion.html**

It works best on a phone — add it to your home screen and it feels like a proper app.
