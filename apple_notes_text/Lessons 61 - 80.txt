I am the light of the world.
This is my only function.   That is why I am here.
62
Forgiveness is my function as the light of the world.
I would fulfill my function that i may be happy.  I would remember this because I want to be happy.
63
The light of the world brings peace to every mind though my forgiveness.
I am the means God has appointed for the salvation of the world.
64
Let me not forget my function.
Let me not try to substitute mine for God’s
Let me forgive and be happy.
This is the world is my function to save.

My function is the one God gave me.
I want not other and I have no other.

Holiness created me holy.
Kindness created me kind.
Helpfulness created me helpful.
Perfection created me perfect.
68
Love holds no grievances.
Shorter:
Love holds no grievances.
Let me not betray myself.
Frequent:
Love holds no grievances

69
My grievances hide the light of the world in me.
My grievances hide the light of the world in me.
I cannot see what I have hidden.
Yet I want to let it be revealed to me for my salvation and the salvation of the world.
If I hold this grievance, the light of the world will be hidden.
70
My salvation comes from me.
My salvation comes from me.
It cannot come from anywhere else.
My salvation comes from me, and only from me.
Nothing outside can hold me back.
71
Only God’s plan for salvation will work.
What would You have me do?
Where would You have me go?
What would You have me say and to whom?
Holding grievances is the opposite of God’s plan for salvation.  And only His plan will work.
72
Holding grievances is an attack on God’s plan for salvation.
What is salvation Father?
I do not know.
Tel me, that I may understand.
Holding grievances is an attack on God’s plan for salvation.
Let me accept it instead.

I will there be light. Let me behold the light that reflects God's Will and mine.
I will there be light. Darkness is not my will.

There is no will but God’s.

77

I am entitled to miracles.
I will not trade miracles for grievances. I want only what belongs to me.
God has established miracles as my right.

Let me behold my saviour in this one You have appointed as the one for me to ask to lead me to the holy light in which he stands, that I may join him.
