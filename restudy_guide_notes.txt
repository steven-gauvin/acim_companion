ReStudyGuide.pdf — NOT a study guide, but a personal email thread

This is a beautiful email correspondence between Steven and Katarina Pilotti (a Swedish opera singer)
from June-September 2024, reconnecting through Sandy (Steven's ex-wife, founder of OnPurpose).

Key quotes from Steven in the emails:
- "Be grateful! Everything teaches something."
- "Remember always that you cannot be anywhere except in the Mind of God."
- "All roads lead to God, my love! He always gently guides us home."
- Steven mentions sending Katarina the ACIM workbook study guides (Part 1 & 2, ~300 pages each)
- Steven mentions making pocket cards for lessons — this is the origin of the flashcard idea!
- "I have in fact written down that today I should send you these study guides."

This is personal correspondence — should NOT be transcribed or included in the app.
But it gives context: Steven's study approach involved reading the lesson, reading the study guide,
doing the lesson, then reading the suggested text. He also made pocket cards to remember lessons.
