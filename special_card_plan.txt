SPECIAL CARD IMPLEMENTATION PLAN
=================================

Approach: Add a lesson 366 as a special "Where Do I Go From Here?" card.
This is the simplest approach - add it as lesson num:366 in the LESSONS array.

The card system works like this:
- LESSONS is an array of objects with {num, title, notes, url, bla_url}
- deck = LESSONS.map((_, i) => i) gives indices [0..364]
- renderCard() reads getCurrentLesson() which is LESSONS[deck[currentIdx]]
- The card front shows "LESSON {num}" and the title
- The card back shows "LESSON {num}", title, and notes
- Navigation: prevCard/nextCard, gotoLesson (1-365 range check)

For the special card:
- num: 366
- title: "Where Do I Go From Here?"
- notes: The full text content from the appendix
- url: could link to the Circle of Atonement or leave as '#'
- bla_url: '#'
